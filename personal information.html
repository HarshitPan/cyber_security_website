Personal Information -:

Personal Information can be considered to include your actual name , your phone number , your address , name of your school , your date of birth , names of your family members , the user name and the password that you set on your computer or Internet websites , etc.


Risks to Personal Information -: If other get your personal information , they can missuse this information to cause harm to you .

For example - They can log on to your e-mail account and send an email to other on your behalf , or they can post undesirable messages or pictures on social networking websites , like Facebook , without your knowledge or approval . Some one may use your phone numbers or address to contact you and can even threaten or harm you .




How to protect your Personal Information -:
Do not post your personal information on websites where general public can view it . Do not post information like your Phone Number , your address and your present location online . Make sure you set the privacy settings on the websites such that only your close friend can see your information .


Passwords -:
An important step to protecting your information online and on your computer is through password . Be caution and follow the best practices given below to make sure passwords and your good habits help to protect your information .



1. Set strong Password : A mistake many people do is to use simple words that often contain their names or other regular words . These are easy to guess and break into by hackers . You should use strong passwords that are long , mix capital and lowercase letters , numbers and symbols .

2. Don't use the same password for different Accounts : Another common mistakes that many people make is that they use the same password for logging onto their computer , their facebook profile and even their bank account . So in this case , if your password is stolen or disclosed to a person who wants to harm you , he or she could get access to all your information with just one password . It is like using the same key for multiple locks . The solution is to use different passwords for different accounts and websites .
